About BrainSpace

Welcome to BrainSpace - a free, fun flashcard tool for optimised learning. Whether you're revising for exams, learning a new language, or preparing for the next pub quiz, BrainSpace is here to help you achieve your goals.

Our Mission

We believe that learning should be engaging and adaptable to suit individual needs, and we strive to empower students with the tools they need to succeed. BrainSpace has been designed with accessibility in mind, with an entirely keyboard-friendly interface, no time pressure on users, and a colour scheme suitable for those with visual impairments. Additionally, the platform is completely free to use, with no ads or hidden costs.

Key Features

  • Spaced Repetition: Learn smarter with scientifically proven methods to retain information longer.
  • Gamification: Stay motivated with points and share your badge collection with friends!
  • Customisable Flashcards: Create, edit, and organise flashcard sets tailored to your needs.

Privacy Policy

We collect your email address only to create and manage your account, enable login, and send account-related notifications like password resets. Your email is securely stored and never shared with third parties. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently deleted.
